# Products Checklist

## Product Display

- Verify product list is displayed
- Verify product names are displayed
- Verify product prices are displayed
- Verify product images are displayed
- Verify product description is displayed
- Verify all available products are displayed

## Product Sorting

- Verify sorting A to Z
- Verify sorting Z to A
- Verify sorting by price low to high
- Verify sorting by price high to low

## Product Actions

- Verify Add to Cart button works
- Verify Remove button works

## UI Verification

- Verify product layout is displayed correctly
- Verify product information is readable
