# Cart Checklist

## Cart Management

- Verify added product appears in cart
- Verify multiple products can be added
- Verify product can be removed
- Verify cart badge updates correctly
- Verify cart badge increases after adding product
- Verify cart badge decreases after removing product
- Verify cart remains empty when no products are added
- Verify cart page shows correct product price
- Verify cart page shows correct product quantity

## Navigation

- Verify Continue Shopping button works
- Verify Checkout button works
- Verify user can navigate back to Products page
- Verify cart presists after navigation back to Product page 

## Cart Validation

- Verify correct product information is displayed
- Verify correct quantity is displayed
